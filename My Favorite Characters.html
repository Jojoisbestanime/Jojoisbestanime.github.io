My Favorite Characters

Iggy The Fool


Iggy is one of my favorite characters because one he is a dog. He is a bit of an asshole to a majority of the Stardust Crusaders but, even though his is a dick he has his good heroic moments by saving a child from a ice shooting hawk, and saving Polnareff. I loved his development as a character over the adventure in Egypt

Joseph Joestar


Joseph Joestar one of my favorite Joestars because he isn't the proper hero. He relies on tricks and lying in order to win. yet he does fight for a just cause he still is just a clown most of the time. Seeing him come back to help other Joestars throughout the series and grow as a character as well makes him one of my favorite Jojos

Jotaro Kujo


I'm a sucker for a badass edgy boy so of course Jotaro will be on my list. He is just such a serious characer that has really cool outfits throughout the series. His fights through the series are insane and very enjoyable. Mostly I enjoy a good badass character I just have a soft spot for them.
